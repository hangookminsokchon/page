Yoolkyu Park

My research focuses on developing mathematical and statistical frameworks for analyzing complex spatial structures in biomedical imaging data. In particular, I study spatial point processes and topological data analysis (TDA) to model cellular organization in high-resolution histopathology images.

I am interested in bridging rigorous theory with real-world biomedical applications, especially through scalable computational implementations that enable reproducible spatial biomarker discovery.

I am a rising senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, pursuing a double major in Mathematics and Statistics & Analytics.
